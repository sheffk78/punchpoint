Features

Built for the field, not the office

Big buttons, high contrast, works in sunlight, works with gloves. The stuff that actually matters on a job site.

GPS Geofencing

Crew can only clock in when they're physically on site. Location is checked in real time. No more clocking in from the truck, the parking lot, or home.

One-Tap Clock In

Select employee, project, and cost code. Clock in takes 5 seconds. GPS captures automatically. Crew gets back to work.

Job Costing

See which jobs are making money and which are bleeding you dry. Budget vs. actual hours and labor costs, side by side. Spot the problem jobs before they eat your margin.

Crew Management

Get your whole crew set up in minutes. Add people one at a time or paste a spreadsheet. Set their rates, track who's active, and you're done. No spreadsheets, no paper, no mess.

Photo Documentation

Photos with GPS and timestamp metadata. Document progress, verify work completed, protect against disputes. Liability protection in your pocket.

Daily Reports

OSHA-compliant daily work logs. Auto-capture weather, crew size, hours, and injuries. Stop handwriting reports at the end of a 12-hour day.

Privacy-First GPS

Location is captured only at clock in and clock out. No continuous tracking. Employees see exactly what's recorded. Trust goes both ways.

Project Geofencing

Drop a pin on each job site and set a radius. Crew can only clock into the project they're standing at. Track hours and labor costs per project, automatically.

Real-Time Dashboard

Active crew count, hours today, active projects, labor cost. Everything you need to run the day, in one view. No digging through menus.

FAQ

Questions contractors ask

Straight answers. No sales talk.

Is my crew's location data private?
Location is captured only at clock in and clock out. No continuous tracking. Employees see exactly what's recorded. GPS coordinates are tied to the time entry, not stored as a live location feed.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
You pick a plan. If you don't choose one, your account automatically drops to the Free plan (up to 3 users). No surprise charges. No locked data.
Can I export my data if I leave?
Yes. Export all time entries, employee data, and project reports as CSV at any time. Your data is yours. We don't hold it hostage.
Does it work offline?
GPS clock-in works offline. Entries sync automatically when the device reconnects to cellular or WiFi. No lost data from dead zones on the job site.
How is this different from busybusy?
No $40/mo admin license fee. AI profitability insights on Premium. Built-in PTO management. Privacy-first GPS (no continuous tracking). REST API on Premium. Same core features, better pricing, no nickel-and-diming.
Do I need to install anything?
No. PunchPoint runs in any mobile browser. Crews open a URL, log in, and clock in. No app store download, no updates to manage, no IT setup.
